Ronto Wrap

Description

The Ronto Wrap is a sandwich made from ronto meat served in a flat piece of bread called a ronto wrapper and topped with slaw and Clutch Sauce. They were available at podracing fanatic Bakkar's Ronto Roasters in Black Spire Outpost on the planet Batuu and were cooked by the pitmaster droid 8D-J8.

The mechanic Salju and Resistance spy Vi Moradi both ordered Ronto Wraps while the former gave the latter a tour of the outpost. 8D-J8 once offered a Ronto Wrap to the Clawdite Remex Io, who turned it down.

Reference

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ½ medium yellow onion, diced
  • ½ pound ground pork
  • 6 ounces chorizo sausag, casings removed
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 14 ounces tomato puree
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ronto wrappers
  • 1 cup guacamole for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large saute pan or skillet pan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until soft, several minutes.

  2. Add the pork and chorizo and cook for about 5 minutes, until the meat is all browned.

  3. Add the brown sugar and tomato puree, and cook for about 15 minutes more, until the sauce is thick.

  4. Season to taste, then spoon into the Ronto Wrappers. Top each with a few dollops of guacamole and enjoy straightaway.
